# Story

Neon Gauntlet follows a young street fighter entering the Metro Arcade district after a network of underground arcade crews starts taking over the city’s neon corridors. Each district has a champion. To advance, the player clears rival waves and defeats the stage boss.

## Demo Objective

Clear Metro Arcade, survive four rivals, trigger the boss, and reach the stage clear point.

## Tone

Teen arcade action: energetic, stylish, dramatic, but not graphic.
