## Commercial Release Rule

Before any production release:

- Every asset must have a source, author, license, and commercial-use status.
- Unknown or unclear assets must be replaced.
- No ripped game sprites, fan-game assets, noncommercial assets, or lookalike protected characters.
- Audio remains covered by the generator and manifests; visual art, maps, bosses, and level-production status still require their own approvals.
